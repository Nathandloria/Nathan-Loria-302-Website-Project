Allegheny College Ultimate

Who are we?

The ultimate frisbee team at Allegheny College is an amazing organization comprised of a womens, men and mixed team. These teams practice together during the fall, winter and spring seasons and participate in tournaments throughout the year. In the spring season, the womens and mens team participate in USAU sanctioned tournaments leading up to sectionals where both teams participate in games against other teams in the same section as Allegheny College. The team is an amzing way to build communication skills, meet and bond with amazing people, be part of a team and overall get involved in the amazing community that is ultimate frisbee.



The Hellbender is the "mascot" for the Allegheny College ultimate frisbee team. A hellbender is a species of aquatic giant salamander that has large populations in French Creek, which is a creek spanning 117 miles, that runs through Meadville, PA which is the city in which Alleghey College is located.
